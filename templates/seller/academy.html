Business 101: Learn Shop Terms

Click the Ask AI about this button on any term card to let the AI explain that concept to you live:

Technology

AI / Artificial Intelligence (कृत्रिम बुद्धिमत्ता)

A smart digital helper that acts like a real person. It listens to your voice commands, understands what you need, and runs your shop tasks automatically.

  • Saves time, reduces spelling mistakes, and helps you work hands-free.
B2B

B2B (व्यापार से व्यापार)

When you sell products in large amounts (bulk) to another shopkeeper or business instead of individual customers.

  • Example: Selling 100 milk packets to a local grocery store.
B2C

B2C (व्यापार से ग्राहक)

When you sell products directly to individual customers who walk into your shop to buy items for their own use.

  • Example: Selling 1 milk packet to a daily customer.
Invoicing

Invoice / Bill (बिल)

A formal paper or digital record showing the items sold, their prices, and the final payment amount due.

  • Serves as proof of sales and is used to track transaction histories.
Inventory

Inventory / Stock (सामान का भंडार)

The total physical items/goods you currently have on hand in your store available for customers to purchase.

  • Tracking stock ensures you never run out of popular items.
Margins

Revenue & Profit (कमाई और मुनाफा)

Revenue is the total cash collected from sales. Profit is what you keep after paying for product costs and rent.

  • Profit is the actual money you take home as salary/income.
Taxation

GST / Taxes (टैक्स)

Goods and Services Tax is a small percentage added to the bill by the government to fund public works.

  • Shopkeepers collect GST from customers and pay it to the government.
Voice Assistant Guidelines

Voice Billing Instructions

Speak clearly to the assistant for fast hands-free billing:

  • Begin invoice statements with **"Bill for [Customer]"**.
  • List items using quantities and names (e.g., **"2 milks and 1 bread"**).
  • Speak clearly and pause slightly after finishing commands to let the auto-submit fire.

Multilingual Tips

Get the best speech recognition results in regional languages:

  • Choose your language from the flag dropdown in the Assistant header.
  • Speak naturally. If you have selected Hindi, you can speak in Hindi or Hinglish!
  • The assistant automatically replies back using matching natural synthesizer voices.
